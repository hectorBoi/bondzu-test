Get to know us!

Bondzù is an education and awareness initiative using the new means of communication that the internet enables. It makes heavy use of social media, smartphone apps, and web pages. What we seek is to give animals in captivity a dignified life. Ideally, animals should live in their habitat; however, there are many animals in captivity that it is impossible to release, because they could not adapt to a wild environment.
