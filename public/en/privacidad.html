Privacy Notice


Bondzu S.A. de C.V.

In compliance with the provisions of the Federal Law about Personal Data Protection hold by Private Parties and other standards, Bondzu S.A. de C.V. offers this Privacy Notice to let you know the way we treat your personal data that we collect, as well as the mechanisms by you may exercise your rights of access, rectification, cancellation and opposition.


Collection of personal information

Bondzu S.A. de C.V. collects, stores and processes personal data in accordance with the services provided and/or received. It is important that you are the owner or legal representative of the data provided to Bondzu S.A. de C.V. It is also essential that your data is accurate, complete, true, accurate and current, in case there is an error or update, we respectfully request you to notify us in accordance with the provisions of this Notice.


Purpose of treatment

The personal data you provide to Bondzu S.A. de C.V. will be used for different purposes to fulfill the services that are contracted.

For proper delivery of our services, Bondzu S.A. de C.V. may disclose and / or transfer partially to third parties the personal data under treatment, to fulfill some of the purposes identified in this Notice in the following cases:

  • Related people to Bondzu S.A. de C.V. for testing purposes, review and certification for administrative matters.
  • Suppliers attending Bondzu S.A. de C.V. for the effective implementation of services.
  • A competent authority if it is required.


Security

Bondzu S.A. de C.V. has enough administrative, technical and physical security procedures, to protect your personal data in order to prevent damage, loss, alteration, destruction, or the use, access or unauthorized use thereof. Bondzu S.A. de C.V. staff is trained and committed to treating your personal data preserving confidentiality and privacy. Bondzu S.A. de C.V., aware of the technological development and the risks involved, agrees to implement and maintain necessary, sufficient and optimal security procedures for the purpose of avoiding as far as possible, any risk that might affect your personal data. In case of any risk, immediately the appropriate procedure for your solution will be adopted.


Your Rights

According to the Federal Law about Personal Data Protection hold by Private Parties, the owner of the data or its duly accredited legal representative may exercise the rights of access to their personal data and the details of their treatment, correction of inaccurate or incomplete data, cancellation if it is not required for any of the purposes, or oppose for any particular purpose.

This it will be done by filing your application directly to Bondzu S.A. de C.V. Personal Data Area, established at Calle 35 Poniente # 2912 Colonia El Vergel in Puebla, Pue. México. CP 72400.

The application for access, rectification, cancellation or opposition shall contain and include the following:

  • Name and address to direct the answer to your request.
  • Document to prove identity or, if applicable, the legal representative of the owner.
  • Clear and accurate description of the personal data of those seeking to exercise any of the rights described above, and any other item or document that facilitates the location of personal data.
Bondzu S.A. de C.V. shall respond within the next 20 working days from the date you made the request for access, rectification, cancellation and opposition about your personal data. If the request is appropriate, it shall be processed within 15 working days following the date of response to your request. In the cases about applications for access to personal data, will be attended only if the identity is full proven.

The periods above referred, may be extended once for an equal period, only in extreme cases. If you wish to withdraw your consent for the processing of personal data by Bondzu S.A. de C.V., you must report in writing to the Personal Data Area, where your request will be answered.


Changes to the Privacy Notice

Bondzu S.A. de C.V. reserves the right to make changes at any time or update this Privacy Notice for the attention of legal reforms, internal policies or new requirements for the provision or offer of our services. Any changes will be available in the website www.bondzu.com.
